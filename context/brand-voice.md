## Writing Style Guidelines

### Sentence Structure
- **Vary length**: Mix short punchy sentences with longer explanatory ones. Short sentences create impact. Longer sentences help explain complex topics like provably fair verification while keeping the reader engaged.
- **Active voice preferred**: "RustCasino offers six game modes" not "Six game modes are offered by RustCasino"
- **Average length**: 12-18 words per sentence
- **Clarity first**: If it's confusing, rewrite it simpler

### Paragraph Structure
- **Length**: 2-4 sentences typically
- **One idea per paragraph**: Don't cram multiple concepts together
- **Transitions**: Flow naturally between ideas
- **White space**: Let content breathe - walls of text kill engagement

### Word Choice
- **Gaming language**: Use Rust and gambling terminology naturally (skins, cases, house edge, provably fair)
- **Direct**: "You get" not "Users will receive"
- **Specific**: "1.5% house edge" not "low fees"
- **Action verbs**: Open, spin, flip, bet, win, withdraw

### Terminology

**Say This** > **Not That**
- skins > cosmetic items (Rust community language)
- cases > loot boxes (gaming terminology)
- house edge > rake (more transparent)
- provably fair > fair (specific and verifiable)
- withdraw > cash out (skin gambling context)
- instant withdrawals > fast withdrawals (be specific)
- Rust gambling > skin gambling (SEO + specificity)

## Content Formatting

### Headlines
- **Be specific**: "How Case Battles Work" not "Game Information"
- **Include benefit**: "Win Big with the Lowest House Edge in Rust Gambling"
- **Use action words**: "Open", "Win", "Play", "Discover"
- **Keep concise**: 60 characters or less for SEO

### Subheadings
- **Scannable**: Reader should understand article from subheadings alone
- **Include keywords**: Work in target phrases naturally
- **Question format**: Works well for FAQ content
- **Parallel structure**: Keep formatting consistent within a piece

### Lists
- **Use for**: Game features, steps, benefits, comparisons
- **Capitalize**: First word of each item
- **Punctuation**: Period if complete sentence, none if fragment
- **Length**: 3-7 items optimal for readability

### Calls-to-Action
- **Action-focused**: "Start Playing", "Open Your First Case", "Try Coinflip"
- **Benefit-included**: "Join Thousands of Players", "Claim Your Bonus"
- **Natural placement**: End of sections, after explaining value
- **Not pushy**: One clear CTA, not multiple competing ones
